Babysitter Requirements

Your Path to Becoming a Trusted Caregiver

Qualifications

  • Minimum age of 18 years
  • High school diploma or equivalent
  • Previous babysitting or childcare experience
  • Completed CPR and First Aid certification

Skills

  • Excellent communication and interpersonal skills
  • Patience and empathy with children
  • Ability to handle emergency situations calmly
  • Basic understanding of child development

Expectations

  • Reliability and punctuality
  • Respect for the family's rules and routines
  • Maintain a safe and engaging environment for children
  • Ability to adapt to different family dynamics
